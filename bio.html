Kirk Jackson's Bio

Kirk was born in San Francisco in 1993.
Since then, he has discovered a few important things about himself.
Namely, that his favorite food is cheese,
favorite animal-themed dance move is the jellyfish walk,
that he doesn't really like peas all that much,
that he loves simple collective dances like the Electric Slide or the Wobble,
that his favorite song is "Scythian Empires" by Andrew Bird,
and finally, that he loves window seats.
